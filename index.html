Chicken Curry With White Rice------>Ksh 1,400

Our world-class Chefs came up with this dish on oue menu as it embodies different concepts of culinary art.

The above dish consists of premium grade chicken sliced in bits and curried creating an amazing choice of meat with flavour that melts in your mouth the minute you take a good bite. It is accompanied with white rice that is sourced from the amazing farms in Thailand. We serve you with fragrant rice garnished with thinly sliced carrots, sliced tomato and cucumber to give you a mouth-watering experience.

Cuenco de cítricos------>Ksh 999

A fruit bowl that consists of citrus that is sourced from the fruit plantations in the Mediterenean Region

Citrus fruits....The most consumed fruits worldwide. Here at flavour House Bar and Restaurant, we decided to plate it and bring an amazing vegetarian option consisting of oranges, lemon, lime, tangerines with grapes.

Vietnamese Pho------>Ksh 1,199

A culinary masterpiece created during the Vietnam War and stuck till date due to its amazing complex flavour

A dish that has become a staple in many asian restaurants is making it's way to Flavour House Bar and Restaurant. It consists of handmade noodles that are premium grade with beef broth as a soup along with slices of beef tenderloin for the tender pieces of meat.

Hummus El Grande------>Ksh 999

An Israeli classic comfort dish with sweet and complex flavours

This dish embodies the different culinary backgrounds of Israel to present a dish that is sweet and loaded with complex flavours that melts in your mouth.

Fruit platter------>Ksh 799
